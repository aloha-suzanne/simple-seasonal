Apples

What to make with apples this fall

Apples are abundant this time of year. Here are some of my favorite ways to use them: in salads with roasted vegetables, leafy greens, and cheese, baked with oats and spices for a delicious healthy dessert, and cooked with squash and then blended into a nourishing soup.

Squash

All about squash!

There are so many varieties of squash! From acorn to butternut to delicata to kabocha, there are endless recipe possibilities. Don't throw away those seeds, you can eat them too!

Late Summer Produce

August's seasonal vegetables

In late August, there is an abundance of tomatoes, eggplant, and zucchini. These vegetables also complement each other and can be cooked together in a variety of ways. I love to grill them with herbs and spices or cook them on a stovetop to make a delicious ratatouille.
